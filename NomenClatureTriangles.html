How to Play

Drag pieces into the ghost triangle. Click/tap a piece to rotate the whole triangle. When the ghost is visible, pieces snap into nearby ghost spaces. Pieces glow neon green only when one of their edges is correctly connected to the matching chemistry edge.

Outer edges contain real chemistry distractors that do not match any other edge.
